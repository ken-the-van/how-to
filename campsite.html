Campsite

Getting ready to stop for the night

Picking a location

  1. Space
    Check for a location that you are with 10m of the power access point and are level
  2. Access to power
    Before setting up camp check the cable can reach the access point
    The blue connector is under the van just in front of the drivers rear left wheel. Connect to the van and then walk and connect to the campsite outlet
  3. Parking on chocks
    This can be hard due to the weight the van.
    1. Get the chocks from the boot on the left hand side
    2. Put the chocks under the front wheels
    3. Slowly drive up until level or on the top step
    4. Use the foot brake and then pull the handbrake up fully
    5. Leave in gear before releasing the foot brake.
  4. Awning
    Ensure there is a few metres space for the awning from the side of the van if you want to use it
