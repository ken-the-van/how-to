Driving

Out and about in Ken

When you are heading out

  1. Unlock the cab
    Press unlock on the remote once
  2. Unlock the rear
    Press unlock on the remote twice
  3. Remove steering lock
    Use the key on the keyring - lock can be stored under the handbrake when driving

Pre-flight Checks

  1. Mirrors
    Check the wing mirrors are pushed out and usable
  2. Hook Up Cable
    Make sure the hook up power cable is removed and inside the van
  3. Air Vents
    Ensure the two roof air vents are both closed
    Press the button on the vent to open and close
  4. Rear Windows
    Ensure the two side windows at the rear are both closed
  5. Child seats
    Ensure the rear child seats are tethered to the floor point
    • Use the cables located on the back of the seats
    • A secure anchor point is located on the floor in front of the fridge
    • First attach the car seat threading the lap belt through its bottom
    • Then attach the anchor cable to the back of the car seat down to the anchor point on the floor behind
  6. Baby Seat
    Use the front seats for the baby car seat

Driving

  1. Ignition
    With a foot on the brake, hold the clutch down and turn the key to start the engine
  2. Handbrake
    Make sure the handbrake is fully down - the dashboard will warn you if not
  3. Changing Gear
    The gearbox feels different to a car and requires a two part movement.

    First put the gear stick into neutral and then into the new gear.
    It feels like a one - two movement and soon becomes familiar.

    Use the gear selection prompt next to the speedometer to help know which gear to use
  4. Braking
    You're almost 3 tonnes so will need ample time to slow down
  5. Cornering
    It's a great drive being up high, just watch out for kerbs as you'll need to go wider to avoid clipping the back tyres
  6. Reversing
    Use the rear view camera on the dashboard that turns on when reversing
    Ensure the cable is plugged into the lighter slot

Controls

  1. Headlights
    There is a dial on the right hand side for sidelights or dipped headlights
  2. Full Beams
    Use the larger stick on the left side.
    • Push forward - Flash
    • Pull back - Full Beams On
  3. Wipers
    Use the larger stick on the left side.
    • Twist - Set wiper speed.
    • Push from end towards wheel - Spray water
  4. Climate Control
    Use the climate control dial to turn on the heaters or fans in the cab. There is no air conditioning so you may need to let the windows down on hot days
  5. Cruise Control
    There is a smaller stick on the left of the steering wheel
    • Pull forward - start cruise control at current speed
    • Push back / brake - stop cruise control
    • Push up - increase speed
    • Push down - decrease speed
  6. Audio
    There is Bluetooth available as well as Radio

    To connect do the following:
    1. Select the bluetooth menu to show 'No Bluetooth audio device connected'
    2. Select 'BT Audio'
    3. Then select bluetooth audio device list
    4. If all three slots are in use, hit right arrow to select on, and then 'De-authorise' to remove it
    5. Then you can select 'Update' on the list of device to scan for your device
    6. When scanning you are prompted to enter a passkey e.g. 0 0 0 0 and then select 'OK'
    7. Your phone should then prompt to connect and enter the same passkey
    8. Now you should see the 'Connected with' message
    9. After you have done this you can use 'Connect' on the main bluetooth menu
    10. You should now be able to play music through the speakers

Parking Up

  1. Flat Ground
    The van is very heavy so avoid parking on hills or the handbrake will not be enough to hold the van
  2. Parking Spaces
    You can bay park into a standard car parking spot. Remember there is a tow bar at the back
    You will need to reverse park and use the reverse camera
  3. Height Restrictions
    You will need 2.4m so will not be able to park in many places with a height restriction
  4. Sun Protection
    Use the sun screens to prevent the cab overheating while you stop
  5. Leave in Gear
    To be safe, first put the van in gear and then switch off the engine.
    If slightly uphill put in first
    If slightly downhill put in reverse
  6. Lock Up
    Use the steering lock to secure the wheel. Press once on the remote to lock the whole van

Troubleshooting

    Key remote does not work
    Fix
    For the drivers door. Pull out the manual key from inside the remote and use that to lock/unlock manually on the drivers side - contact us to sort out replacing battery
    You will need to lock the side door and passengers side door using the push down locks on the inside of the door then closing them.
    Roof air vents do not lower when pressing the button
    Fix
    Twist the black vent knobs to manually lower the vent until it is closed
    Issues with reversing camera
    Fix
    Check for mud or dust on the camera located above the rear number plate.
    Note: The camera is activated through bluetooth so can be triggered by other vehicles when driving on motorways if they are using the same device
