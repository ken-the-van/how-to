Driving

Out and about in Ken

When you are heading out

  1. Unlock the cab
    Use the key remote unlock button to unlock the van
    In the case the battery is dead pull out the manual key from the remote
  2. Unlock the rear
    Press the unlock again to unlock the side door to the van
  3. Remove steering lock
    Disable the anti-theft steering wheel lock with its key on the keyring

Checks

  1. Mirrors
    Check the wing mirrors are correctly set
    !
  2. Hook Up Cable
    Check the hook up power cable is not attached
    !
  3. Air Vents
    Ensure the roof air vents are down
    !
  4. Ignition
    With a foot on the brake, hold the clutch down and turn the key to start the engine

Parking Up

  1. Flat Ground
    The van is very heavy so aim to park on flat ground ideally with plenty of space
    !
  2. Leave in Gear
    To be safe, leave the van in gear and then switch off the engine
    !
  3. Chocks
    There are chocks in the boot to level up slightly when at campsites Drive onto them with the front wheels
  4. Reversing Camera
    Plug in the rear view camera cable to the lighter slot and use the dashboard screen
  5. Lock Up
    Use the steering lock to secure the wheel. Then one press on the remote to lock the whole van
    !
